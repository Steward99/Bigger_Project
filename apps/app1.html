Description :

This is a simple to do list application. Simply write or insert your to do list into the text box, then hit the ⭕ button and it will create a new to do list for you. You can toggle the ✔ button in order to check the list that have done. In order to delete the list, you can just simply push the ✖ button. Please do enjoy this simple to do list application 😁😁 thank you!!💛💙

NOTE :

because this is just a demo version and also just for fun, your lists wont be saved if you exit the browser.
